PRIMORDIAL DEMONS

転生したらスライムだった件

Tensei shitara Slime Datta Ken

について About

In the fantastical world of That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime, Primordial Demons emerge as one of the most enigmatic and formidable entities. We first get to know about them through Diablo, a primordial demon that Rimuru accidentally summoned. As the story progressed, we’ve come to realize how powerful, dangerous, and cunning he and his kind truly is.

This article delves into the depths of these fascinating beings, shedding light on the Primordial Demons, their identities, unparalleled power, and their role in the series. Join us as we unravel the secrets surrounding these ancient and influential beings.

―✲メンバー members

Primordial Red ● Guy Crimson

レッドプライモーディアル

Rouge, now known as Guy Crimson, is the first and most powerful among the Primordial Daemons. He proudly declares himself the first Demon Lord and possesses an overbearing personality. Guy is highly confident and doesn't hesitate to use coercion and threats to enforce his beliefs. He is recognizable by his flowing crimson red hair and eyes. Guy Crimson is loyal only to himself and currently resides in the Ice Continent as its ruler.

Primordial Black ● Diablo

黒の原始

Noir, now known as Diablo, is the Black Primordial. He is often considered an oddball among daemons due to his different values, particularly regarding the pursuit of power. After being summoned by Rimuru Tempest, Noir immediately pledged his undying loyalty and remains fiercely devoted to him. He is distinguished by his signature full butler attire with white gloves, as well as black hair with a strand of red in the front. Diablo is loyal to Rimuru Tempest and currently resides in the Jura Tempest Federation.

Primordial White ● Testarossa

ホワイト・プリモーディアル

Blanc, now known as Testarossa, is the White Primordial. Despite her gentle appearance, she possesses one of the most terrifying personalities among the Daemons. In the Lake Shore Dyed Scarlet Incident, she massacred an entire city to avenge a friend. After swearing loyalty to Rimuru Tempest, Testarossa gets involved in political manipulations, coercing other members of the Western states council to further her goals. She is distinguished by her silver hair, snow-white skin, and red eyes. Testarossa is loyal to Rimuru Tempest and currently resides in the Jura Tempest Federation.

Primordial Yellow ● Carrera

イエロープライモーディアル

Jaune, now known as Carrera, is the Yellow Primordial. Prior to coming under Rimuru's guidance, she had a hobby of sporadically unleashing bursts of Nuclear Magic, often directed towards Leon's capital in El Dorado. Although she has seemingly calmed down since then, those who knew her before still view her as a menace. Carrera is distinguished by her short blonde hair and blue eyes. Carrera is loyal to Rimuru Tempest and currently resides in the Jura Tempest Federation.

Primordial Purple ● Ultima

原始の紫

Violet, now known as Ultima, is the Purple Primordial. Despite her youthful appearance, she takes pleasure in being treated like a princess by her subordinate Daemons. However, she also exhibits a fondness for inflicting pain on others. After being convinced by Noir, Ultima becomes loyal to Rimuru. She can be recognized by her purple hair with a side ponytail and her yellow eyes. Ultima is loyal to Rimuru Tempest and currently resides in the Jura Tempest Federation.

Primordial Green ● Misery

緑の原始

Vert, now known as Misery, is the Green Primordial. As a maid alongside Rain under Rouge's command, she takes her responsibilities very seriously and dedicates herself entirely to her master. Misery goes to the extent of establishing a cult centered around aiding Rouge's observations of humans. She is characterized by her long, curly green hair and eyes, and she wears a deep-red maid outfit. Misery is loyal to Guy Crimson and currently resides in the Ice Continent.

Primordial Blue ● Rain

ブルー・プリモーディアル

Bleu, now known as Rain, is the Blue Primordial. Despite her composed and beautiful appearance, she dislikes working and often leaves tasks to Vert. Even in the midst of heated battles, Rain tends to complain about various things. She maintains unwavering loyalty to Rouge. Rain is identified by her short blue hair and eyes, and she wears a deep-red maid outfit. Rain is loyal to Guy Crimson and currently resides in the Ice Continent.
